My name is Laura Joy and my love of jewellery combined with a passion for designing, has encouraged me to develop my own range of jewellery, and create my company, ‘Elljoys’.

The aim of Elljoys is to create items of jewellery that instead of being bought ‘off the shelf’, have actually been chosen and designed by my customers, therefore becoming more meaningful and special to them.

All of the jewellery is made to order after you have chosen the design and colour scheme at a consultation and can be made as matching sets or individual pieces.

I use mainly silver or gold plated metals, invisible thread or wires along with glass or acrylic beads, in various colours and styles.

My aim is to make every design the customer wants.
